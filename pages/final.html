And Then, We Stayed

Ek metro station se shuru hui kahani… ab shaadi तक पहुँच रही है. And maybe that’s what makes us special. Not because our story was perfect — it wasn’t. We had distance. We had misunderstandings. Busy days. Silent nights. Missed calls. Delayed flights. Tough phases that almost exhausted us. But somehow, through every version of life, we kept finding our way back to each other. From a hug at Dwarka Sector 21, to countless station goodbyes, to mountains, airports, screens, countdowns, and long-distance nights — we built something real. Something patient. Something stubborn. Something that stayed. And now, after four years of choosing each other again and again, we’re finally walking towards forever. Next year, there won’t be return tickets. No more countdowns till the next meeting. No more “see you soon.” Just us. Coming home to each other, every single day. "Safar khoobsurat tha… par manzil tum ho." और शायद प्यार यही होता है — बार-बार उसी इंसान को चुनना, हर मुश्किल के बाद भी। "तुमसे मोहब्बत थी, तुमसे मोहब्बत है, और शायद… हमेशा रहेगी।" Next year, I won’t just be meeting you again. I’ll be marrying my best friend, my safest place, and the greatest thing that ever happened to me.
